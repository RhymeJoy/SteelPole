About us

We have upheld our passion and commitment to fine cuisine for over a decade, consistently serving every guest with fresh and delicious dishes. Each dish is prepared with carefully selected ingredients and meticulous cooking, preserving the natural flavors and aromas.

  • Carefully selected free-range chicken, tender and juicy.
  • Seasoned with local spices and our signature sauce for a rich aroma.
  • Grilled with our steel-pole charcoal technique, ensuring even heat — crispy skin with succulent meat.

Each dish is crafted with care to keep its authentic flavors and aromas. In our cozy restaurant, whether it’s a family dinner or a gathering with friends, you’ll always enjoy a truly delicious experience.

Roasted Steel-Pole Chicken

Lin Junyi's Signature Roasted Chicken: Prepared with a whole fresh chicken, carefully marinated and slow-roasted using the unique “steel tube” charcoal method. During roasting, the natural juices are perfectly sealed in, resulting in crispy golden skin and tender, succulent meat inside. Every bite releases a captivating aroma, blending the natural fragrance of chicken with the richness of the marinade for layers of flavor. Whether enjoyed on its own or as the centerpiece of a shared meal, this hearty dish showcases the chef’s dedication and is a must-try signature specialty.

Stir-fried Wild Short Necked Clams

Wild Short Necked Clams: Carefully selected wild-caught sea clams, freshly harvested with firm, plump meat. Lightly stir-fried or simply steamed, they retain their natural sweetness and pure ocean flavor. Each bite bursts with rich marine aroma and a pleasantly springy texture. Low in fat yet full of taste, this dish is a favorite among seafood lovers. Whether enjoyed on its own or paired with a light soup, it captures the essence of Taiwan’s coastal flavors — perfect for those who appreciate freshness in its purest form.

Shark Fin Broth

Carefully selected top-quality shark fin is paired with fresh seafood and silky eggs, then slowly simmered to perfection. The broth is rich and flavorful, with the shark fin fully absorbing the essence of the soup. Every spoonful delivers the natural sweetness of seafood and a deep, savory aroma. Garnished with fresh vegetables, the dish offers layers of refined taste while preserving the essence of traditional flavors. Ideal for banquets or family gatherings, it reflects the chef’s dedication to ingredients and technique — a true classic among Chinese seafood soups.

Dongpo Pork

Premium pork belly with skin is slow-braised until meltingly tender — rich yet never greasy, with a soft, elastic skin. Simmered in a secret sauce with traditional Chinese spices, each piece absorbs deep, savory aromas and develops a glossy, enticing red hue. Every bite delivers layers of flavor: the fragrance of the sauce, the silky smoothness of the fat, and the satisfying texture of the meat. Sweet yet balanced, it leaves a lingering aftertaste. More than just a banquet delicacy, Dongpo Pork is a classic of traditional Chinese cuisine, showcasing masterful control of heat and ingredients.

Omelette with Fresh Shrimp and Jade Sauce

Fresh diced shrimp are folded into a silky omelet, then enhanced with a glossy green vegetable sauce for added color and texture. The eggs are fragrant and tender, while the shrimp remain sweet and springy. The jade sauce adds both sheen and a smooth finish, making the dish beautifully balanced in flavor, aroma, and presentation. Perfect on its own or paired with rice, it’s a refined home-style favorite that is both nourishing and delicious.
